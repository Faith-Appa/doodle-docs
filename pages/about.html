Info on the Site

Doodle Docs is a website I created for my CIS155 class. My professor wanted us to create a site that we would actually use or become helpful to us in the future.

I chose to create a site where I can put my creative work out there. This site will serve as a catalyst for future reference.

Another thing about this site is that its here to help others! I am no master but I think its neat to learn alongside fellow creatives.

Who am I?

My name is Faith, and I'm a student at Olympic College. I have a passion for art and design, which is showcased here!

I have a dream, and this site and content is just the stepping stone to making that dream come true!

I would like to create art whether thats comics, illustrations, or graphic design. Something else I would love to achieve with my creativity is combining it with my personal faith.
