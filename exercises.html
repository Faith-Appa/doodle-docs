Gestures

Drawing and seeing!

Gestures

Gestures are just glorified stick figures. The thing to really focus on with these is the understanding basic anatomy and how bodies look in simple poses. Practice drawing figures in simple standing or sitting poses.

Gestures

Value

Shading and light!

Value

Value is the scale of lightness to darkness. It is an important element of art that helps to create depth, contrast, and emphasis in a drawing. Understanding how to use value effectively can help you create more realistic and dynamic drawings.

Value

Observation

Observation

Observation is the study of the world around you. Then applying that to your drawings, not drawing what you think you see.

Observation

Anatomy

Drawing the human body!

Anatomy

Anatomy is the key to drawing realistic figures. Especially ones that look correct. This applies to all things that have a skeleton! When digging into anatomy start with one section first, do not attempt to do a indepth study of the whole body.
